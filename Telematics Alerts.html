Alerts

A range of alerts designed to notify you via pop-up, email or SMS for various exceptional or emergency situations.

The different kinds for Alerts provided on FleetMan are as follows:

01

Stoppage Alert

This alert is generated when the vehicle stops/ stays at any location beyond the 'Stoppage' Limit assigned by you.

02

Over speed Alert

This alert is generated when the vehicle speed exceeds beyond the 'Overspeed' Limit assigned by you.

03

Ignition On/Off Alert

This alert is generated whenever vehicle Ignition is made ON or OFF

04

Secondary Battery Source Alert

This alert is generated when the power supply coming to Telematics Unit from main battery of vehicle is disconnected.

05

Continuous Driving Alert (Beyond defined limit time)

This alert is generated when the vehicle is running beyond defined time limit.

06

Night Driving Alert

This alert is generated if the vehicle moves in between the certain night period defined by you when your vehicle should not be moving.

07

Idling Alert

This alert is generated if ignition key remains in power ON position when vehicle is stationary beyond provided limit.

08

Geo-Fence Entry/ Exit Alert

This alert is generated when your vehicle enters or exits through the geo-fence assigned to it.

09

Delivery Compliance Report

This alert is generated for the exceptions occurred while your vehicle running on certain assigned trip. The exceptions might be delay at intermediate point, trip start, trip end etc.

10

Harsh Braking Alert

This alert is generated when the vehicle is decelerated at a dangerous rate. The thresholds have been defined in the system

11

Harsh Acceleration Alert

This alert is generated when the vehicle is accelerated at a dangerous rate. The thresholds have been defined in the system. This can be accessed through the Menu bar, below 'Alerts Settings' you can find following options: Select / Edit Alerts, De-activate Alert, View Alert Settings
